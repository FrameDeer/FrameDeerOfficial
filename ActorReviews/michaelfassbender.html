Michael Fassbender

Date of Birth: April 2, 1977

Location of Birth: Heidelberg, West Germany

Height: 6'0" (183 cm)

Michael Fassbender is a German-Irish actor known for his intense and transformative performances across independent films and major blockbusters. With his commanding screen presence and versatility, he has established himself as one of the most respected actors of his generation, equally adept at dramatic roles and action franchises.

Early Career

Fassbender began his acting career in British television, with notable roles in series like Band of Brothers (2001) and Hex (2004). His breakthrough film role came in 300 (2006) as the Spartan warrior Stelios. However, it was his powerful performance as Bobby Sands in Hunger (2008) that brought him critical acclaim and established him as a serious dramatic actor. His collaboration with director Steve McQueen continued with Shame (2011), which earned him widespread recognition and solidified his reputation as one of the most daring and committed actors working in cinema.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actor – 12 Years a Slave (2014)
  • BAFTA Award Nomination for Best Actor – Hunger (2008)
  • Golden Globe Award Nomination for Best Actor – Shame (2012)
  • Volpi Cup for Best Actor – Shame (2011)
  • British Independent Film Award for Best Actor – Hunger (2008)
  • European Film Award for Best Actor – Shame (2011)
  • Irish Film & Television Award for Best Actor – Hunger (2008)
  • Saturn Award Nomination for Best Actor – Prometheus (2012)
