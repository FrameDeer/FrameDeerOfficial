Robert Downey Jr.

Date of Birth: April 4, 1965

Location of Birth: Manhattan, New York City, U.S.

Height: 5'8" (173 cm)

Robert Downey Jr. is an Academy Award-winning actor known for his charismatic performances and remarkable career resurgence. From troubled early years to becoming one of Hollywood's highest-paid actors, he has established himself as a versatile performer capable of both dramatic depth and comedic timing.

Early Career

Robert Downey Jr. began his acting career as a child, appearing in his father's films. He gained recognition in the 1980s with roles in Weird Science (1985) and Less Than Zero (1987). His breakthrough came with an Academy Award nomination for his portrayal of Charlie Chaplin in Chaplin (1992). Despite early success, his career was derailed by substance abuse issues throughout the 1990s and early 2000s. His remarkable comeback began with roles in Gothika (2003) and Kiss Kiss Bang Bang (2005), culminating in his career-defining role as Tony Stark/Iron Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor – Oppenheimer (2024)
  • BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role – Oppenheimer (2024)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actor – Oppenheimer (2024)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Sherlock Holmes (2010)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Cast – Oppenheimer (2024)
  • People's Choice Award for Favorite Movie Actor (multiple years)
