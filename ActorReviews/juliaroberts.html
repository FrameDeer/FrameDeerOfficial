Julia Roberts

Date of Birth: October 28, 1967

Location of Birth: Smyrna, Georgia, U.S.

Height: 5'9" (175 cm)

Julia Roberts is one of the most successful and highest-paid actresses in Hollywood history, known for her megawatt smile, charismatic screen presence, and remarkable versatility. With an Academy Award win and numerous blockbuster successes, she has remained one of America's most beloved movie stars for over three decades.

Early Career

Julia Roberts began her acting career in the late 1980s with small roles in films before her breakthrough performance in Mystic Pizza (1988). Her career skyrocketed with Steel Magnolias (1989), which earned her first Academy Award nomination, and the global phenomenon Pretty Woman (1990), which made her an international superstar and earned her second Oscar nomination. Throughout the 1990s, Roberts established herself as Hollywood's premier leading lady with successful films like Sleeping with the Enemy (1991), The Pelican Brief (1993), and My Best Friend's Wedding (1997). Her career reached its artistic peak with her Academy Award-winning performance in Erin Brockovich (2000), cementing her status as both a commercial powerhouse and critically acclaimed actress.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Actress – Erin Brockovich (2001)
  • BAFTA Award for Best Actress – Erin Brockovich (2001)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Erin Brockovich (2001)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Pretty Woman (1991)
  • Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress – Steel Magnolias (1990)
  • Academy Award nomination for Best Actress – Pretty Woman (1991)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance – Erin Brockovich (2001)
  • People's Choice Award for Favorite Movie Actress (multiple years)
  • MTV Movie Award for Best Female Performance – Pretty Woman (1991)
  • GLAAD Media Award for Outstanding Film – The Normal Heart (2014)
