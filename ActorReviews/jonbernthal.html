Jon Bernthal

Date of Birth: September 20, 1976

Location of Birth: Washington, D.C., USA

Height: 5'11" (180 cm)

Jon Bernthal is an American actor known for his intense, physically commanding performances and his ability to portray complex, morally ambiguous characters. With his distinctive gravelly voice and raw emotional intensity, he has become one of Hollywood's most sought-after actors for roles requiring both physical prowess and deep emotional vulnerability.

Early Career

Jon Bernthal began his acting career in the early 2000s after studying at the Moscow Art Theatre and graduating from Harvard University's Institute for Advanced Theater Training. His early work included guest roles on television shows like Boston Public and CSI: Miami, along with small film roles. His breakthrough came with his portrayal of Shane Walsh in AMC's critically acclaimed series The Walking Dead (2010-2012), where his complex performance as Rick Grimes' best friend turned antagonist earned him widespread recognition. This role launched him into higher-profile film work, including Martin Scorsese's The Wolf of Wall Street (2013) and David Ayer's Fury (2014). He further cemented his status with his iconic portrayal of Frank Castle/The Punisher in the Marvel Netflix series, a role he would reprise in the standalone series The Punisher (2017-2019).
