María Pedraza

Date of Birth: January 26, 1996

Location of Birth: Madrid, Spain

Height: 5'7" (170 cm)

María Pedraza is a Spanish actress and former ballet dancer who gained international recognition for her role as Alison Parker in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (La Casa de Papel). Known for her elegant presence, emotional depth, and background in dance, she has become one of Spain's most promising young actresses with a rapidly growing international career.

Early Career

María Pedraza began her artistic career as a professional ballet dancer, studying at the Royal Conservatory of Dance in Madrid before transitioning to acting. Her breakthrough came with her film debut in the Netflix original film Elite (2018), where she played Marina Nunier, a role that showcased her dramatic range and established her as a rising star. Her international recognition expanded with her portrayal of Alison Parker in Money Heist (2017-2021), where her character's kidnapping and subsequent Stockholm syndrome became central to the series' first heist. Pedraza's background in ballet has informed her physical acting style, bringing grace and precision to her performances. She has continued to build her career with roles in both Spanish and international productions, demonstrating her versatility across different genres and formats.
