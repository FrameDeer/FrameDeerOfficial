Miguel Herrán

Date of Birth: April 25, 1996

Location of Birth: Málaga, Spain

Height: 5'9" (175 cm)

Miguel Herrán is a Spanish actor who rose to international fame for his role as Aníbal Cortés (Rio) in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (La Casa de Papel). Known for his youthful energy, emotional vulnerability, and natural screen presence, he has become one of Spain's most promising young actors with a career spanning both television and film.

Early Career

Miguel Herrán began his acting career with his breakthrough role in the critically acclaimed film A Cambio de Nada (2015), for which he won the Goya Award for Best New Actor. This early success established him as a talented young actor to watch. His international breakthrough came with his portrayal of Rio in Money Heist (2017-2021), where he played the youngest member of the heist crew, a skilled hacker whose relationship with Tokyo became one of the series' central emotional arcs. Simultaneously, he gained further recognition for his role as Christian in the Netflix series Elite (2018-2020), showcasing his versatility across different genres. Herrán's ability to portray youthful innocence, technological expertise, and emotional depth has made him one of Spain's most successful young actors of his generation.
