Álvaro Morte

Date of Birth: February 23, 1975

Location of Birth: Algeciras, Cádiz, Spain

Height: 6'1" (185 cm)

Álvaro Morte is a Spanish actor who gained international fame for his portrayal of Sergio Marquina (The Professor) in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (La Casa de Papel). Known for his intellectual intensity and charismatic screen presence, he has become one of Spain's most recognized actors worldwide, bringing depth and complexity to his characters.

Early Career

Álvaro Morte began his acting career in Spanish television, appearing in various series including Hospital Central and El secreto de Puente Viejo. He studied dramatic art at the School of Dramatic Art in Córdoba and later moved to Madrid to pursue his acting career. His breakthrough came with his role as The Professor in Money Heist (2017-2021), which transformed him from a respected Spanish television actor into an international star. The series became Netflix's most-watched non-English language series and one of the most-watched series overall, earning him global recognition. Following this success, he has continued to work in both Spanish and international productions, demonstrating his versatility across different genres and languages.
