Pedro Alonso

Date of Birth: June 21, 1971

Location of Birth: Vigo, Galicia, Spain

Height: 6'0" (183 cm)

Pedro Alonso is a Spanish actor who gained international recognition for his compelling portrayal of Andrés de Fonollosa (Berlin) in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (La Casa de Papel). Known for his sophisticated charm, aristocratic demeanor, and ability to portray complex anti-heroes, he has become one of Spain's most distinguished character actors.

Early Career

Pedro Alonso began his acting career in the 1990s, studying at the Royal School of Dramatic Art in Madrid. He built his reputation through extensive work in Spanish television, appearing in numerous series including Mareas Vivas, Padre Casares, and Gran Hotel. His breakthrough role came with his portrayal of Berlin in Money Heist (2017-2021), where his character's sophisticated villainy and complex morality captivated audiences worldwide. Despite his character's death in the early seasons, his popularity led to his return in flashbacks and eventually his own spin-off series. Alonso's performance showcased his ability to bring depth and humanity to morally ambiguous characters, establishing him as an international star and one of the most memorable aspects of the global phenomenon.
