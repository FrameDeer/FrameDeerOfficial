Morgan Freeman

Date of Birth: June 1, 1937

Location of Birth: Memphis, Tennessee, USA

Height: 6'2" (188 cm)

Morgan Freeman is an Academy Award-winning actor and narrator known for his distinctive voice and authoritative presence. With a career spanning over five decades, he has become one of Hollywood's most respected and recognizable figures, appearing in numerous critically acclaimed and commercially successful films.

Early Career

Morgan Freeman began his acting career in theater, making his Broadway debut in 1967's Hello, Dolly! He gained early recognition as a member of the PBS children's television series The Electric Company (1971-1977), where he developed his distinctive vocal delivery. His film career started with small roles in the 1970s and 1980s, but his breakthrough came in 1987 with his Oscar-nominated performance in Street Smart. This led to his iconic role as Hoke Colburn in Driving Miss Daisy (1989), which earned him international acclaim and established him as a leading dramatic actor in Hollywood.
