Saoirse Ronan

Date of Birth: April 12, 1994

Location of Birth: The Bronx, New York City, USA

Height: 5'6" (168 cm)

Saoirse Ronan is a four-time Academy Award-nominated Irish-American actress renowned for her exceptional range, emotional depth, and compelling screen presence. Known for her collaborations with acclaimed directors and her ability to portray complex characters across different eras and genres, she has established herself as one of the most talented and respected actors of her generation.

Early Career

Saoirse Ronan began her acting career in Irish television, with her breakthrough coming in Joe Wright's Atonement (2007), where her performance as the manipulative Briony Tallis earned her an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress at just 13 years old, making her one of the youngest nominees in Oscar history. She continued to build her reputation with roles in The Lovely Bones (2009) and Hanna (2011), showcasing her versatility. Her career reached new heights with her collaborations with director Wes Anderson in The Grand Budapest Hotel (2014) and particularly with her leading role in Brooklyn (2015), which earned her first Best Actress Oscar nomination. This was followed by two more Best Actress nominations for Lady Bird (2017) and Little Women (2019), cementing her status as a premier dramatic actress.
