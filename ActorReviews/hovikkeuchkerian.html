Hovik Keuchkerian

Date of Birth: November 14, 1972

Location of Birth: Beirut, Lebanon

Height: 6'2" (188 cm)

Hovik Keuchkerian is a Spanish actor, former boxer, and writer of Armenian-Lebanese descent known for his powerful physical presence and compelling character performances. Gaining international recognition for his role in Money Heist, he has established himself as one of Spain's most distinctive character actors with his unique blend of physicality and emotional depth.

Early Career

Hovik Keuchkerian began his career as a professional boxer before transitioning to acting and writing. He made his acting debut in the early 2000s and quickly became known for his distinctive look and powerful screen presence. His breakthrough came with roles in Spanish films like The Night of the Sunflowers (2006) and Even the Rain (2010). Keuchkerian gained international fame for his portrayal of Bogotá in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (2019-2021), where his character's loyalty and strength became fan favorites. In addition to his acting career, he is an accomplished writer and poet, having published several works that showcase his literary talents alongside his acting prowess.
