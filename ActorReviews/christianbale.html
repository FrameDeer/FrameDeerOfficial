Christian Bale

Date of Birth: January 30, 1974

Location of Birth: Haverfordwest, Pembrokeshire, Wales

Height: 6'0" (183 cm)

Christian Bale is an Academy Award-winning actor renowned for his intense method acting and dramatic physical transformations for roles. With a career spanning from childhood stardom to acclaimed adult performances, he has established himself as one of the most dedicated and versatile actors of his generation.

Early Career

Christian Bale began his acting career as a child actor, making his film debut at age 13 in Steven Spielberg's Empire of the Sun (1987), for which he received widespread critical acclaim. He continued working in film and television throughout his teenage years, including roles in Newsies (1992) and Little Women (1994). His breakthrough adult role came as Patrick Bateman in American Psycho (2000), which established him as a serious actor willing to take on challenging and transformative roles.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor – The Fighter (2011)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actor – The Fighter (2011)
  • Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Actor – American Psycho (2001)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Cast – The Fighter (2011)
  • Empire Award for Best Actor – The Dark Knight (2009)
