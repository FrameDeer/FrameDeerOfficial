Rodrigo de la Serna

Date of Birth: April 18, 1976

Location of Birth: Buenos Aires, Argentina

Height: 5'9" (175 cm)

Rodrigo de la Serna is an acclaimed Argentine actor known for his charismatic screen presence and remarkable versatility across film, television, and theatre. Gaining international recognition for his role in The Motorcycle Diaries and later in Money Heist, he has established himself as one of Latin America's most respected and internationally recognized actors.

Early Career

Rodrigo de la Serna began his acting career in Argentine theatre and television in the late 1990s. His breakthrough came with his acclaimed performance as Alberto Granado in Walter Salles' The Motorcycle Diaries (2004), which brought him international attention and critical praise. He continued to build his reputation with roles in Argentine cinema and television, becoming one of the country's most prominent actors. His international profile expanded significantly when he joined the cast of Money Heist as Palermo in the later seasons, bringing his signature charm and complexity to the global hit series. Throughout his career, de la Serna has demonstrated his ability to navigate between commercial success and artistic integrity, working across different genres and languages.
