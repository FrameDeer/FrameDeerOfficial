Najwa Nimri

Date of Birth: February 14, 1972

Location of Birth: Pamplona, Navarre, Spain

Height: 5'6" (168 cm)

Najwa Nimri is a Spanish actress and singer who gained international recognition for her role as Alicia Sierra in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (La Casa de Papel). Known for her intense screen presence, complex character portrayals, and distinctive artistic style, she has established herself as one of Spain's most versatile and respected artists across film, television, and music.

Early Career

Najwa Nimri began her career in the 1990s, initially gaining recognition as a musician with her band Najwajean before transitioning to acting. Her breakthrough film role came in Alejandro Amenábar's The Others (2001), which brought her international attention. She built a successful career in Spanish cinema with notable performances in films like Sex and Lucia (2001) and Bullet in the Head (2008). Her television career flourished with roles in series like Vis a Vis (Locked Up) (2015-2019), where she played the formidable prison director Zulema Zahir. Her international stardom was cemented with her portrayal of the pregnant inspector Alicia Sierra in Money Heist (2019-2021), where her character's ruthless determination and complex morality made her one of the series' most compelling antagonists.
