Tom Hanks

Date of Birth: July 9, 1956

Location of Birth: Concord, California, USA

Height: 6'0" (183 cm)

Tom Hanks is one of Hollywood's most beloved and respected actors, known for his everyman charm and remarkable versatility. A two-time Academy Award winner for Best Actor, he has starred in some of the most iconic films of the past four decades, spanning comedy, drama, and historical epics.

Early Career

Tom Hanks began his acting career in theater and television after studying theater at Chabot College and California State University. His first major role was on the ABC sitcom Bosom Buddies (1980-1982), where he gained recognition for his comedic timing. He transitioned to film with roles in horror movies like He Knows You're Alone (1980) and the Disney comedy Splash (1984), which became his breakthrough role. Throughout the 1980s, he established himself as a talented comedic actor in films like Bachelor Party (1984) and Big (1988), the latter earning him his first Academy Award nomination and marking his transition to more serious roles.
