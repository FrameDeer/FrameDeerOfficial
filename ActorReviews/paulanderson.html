Paul Anderson

Date of Birth: February 12, 1978

Location of Birth: Newcastle upon Tyne, England

Height: 5'10" (178 cm)

Paul Anderson is an English actor known for his intense character work, distinctive screen presence, and remarkable ability to portray complex, often morally ambiguous characters. Best known for his role as Arthur Shelby Jr. in the hit series Peaky Blinders, he has established himself as one of Britain's most compelling character actors with a diverse range of performances across television and film.

Early Career

Paul Anderson began his acting career in theatre before transitioning to screen roles. He made his film debut in The Football Factory (2004) and built his reputation through supporting roles in British films and television series. His breakthrough came with his critically acclaimed performance in The Firm (2009), a television adaptation of the 1988 film. Anderson gained international recognition for his role as Arthur Shelby Jr. in the BBC series Peaky Blinders (2013-2022), which showcased his ability to portray complex, volatile characters with depth and authenticity. His film career expanded with roles in major productions like The Revenant (2015) and Legend (2015), cementing his status as a versatile and compelling character actor.
