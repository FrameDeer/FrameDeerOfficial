Keanu Reeves

Date of Birth: September 2, 1964

Location of Birth: Beirut, Lebanon

Height: 6'1" (186 cm)

Keanu Reeves is one of Hollywood's most beloved and enduring actors, known for his iconic action roles, philosophical demeanor, and remarkable humility. With a career spanning over three decades, he has created some of cinema's most memorable characters while maintaining a reputation as one of the kindest and most genuine figures in the entertainment industry.

Early Career

Keanu Reeves began his acting career in the mid-1980s with small roles in Canadian television and films. His breakthrough came with the teen comedy Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure (1989), which established him as a rising star and created his first iconic character. Throughout the early 1990s, he demonstrated his range with diverse roles in My Own Private Idaho (1991), Point Break (1991), and Speed (1994), transitioning from comedic roles to action star. However, it was his role as Neo in The Matrix (1999) that cemented his status as a global superstar and created one of the most influential film franchises in history. Despite massive success, Reeves has maintained his humble persona and continued to take on challenging roles in both independent films and major blockbusters.
