Robert Pattinson

Date of Birth: May 13, 1986

Location of Birth: London, England

Height: 6'1" (185 cm)

Robert Pattinson is an English actor known for his versatility and transformative performances. Rising to global fame as Edward Cullen in the Twilight saga, he has since established himself as one of the most daring and respected actors of his generation through his work in independent and arthouse cinema.

Early Career

Robert Pattinson began his acting career in London's theatre scene before making his screen debut in 2005's Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire as Cedric Diggory. His breakthrough came in 2008 when he was cast as Edward Cullen in Twilight, which launched him to international stardom. Following the massive success of the Twilight franchise, Pattinson deliberately shifted toward independent and challenging roles, working with acclaimed directors like David Cronenberg, the Safdie Brothers, and Claire Denis to reinvent himself as a serious actor capable of complex character work.
