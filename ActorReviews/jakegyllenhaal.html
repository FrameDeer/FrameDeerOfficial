Jake Gyllenhaal

Date of Birth: December 19, 1980

Location of Birth: Los Angeles, California, USA

Height: 5'11" (180 cm)

Jake Gyllenhaal is an Academy Award-nominated actor known for his intense and transformative performances across a wide range of genres. With a career spanning over two decades, he has established himself as one of Hollywood's most versatile and dedicated actors, consistently choosing challenging and complex roles.

Early Career

Gyllenhaal began his acting career as a child actor, making his film debut in City Slickers (1991). He gained early recognition for his role in October Sky (1999), but his breakthrough came with his performance as a troubled teenager in Donnie Darko (2001), which became a cult classic. His career reached new heights with his Oscar-nominated role as Jack Twist in Brokeback Mountain (2005), which established him as a serious dramatic actor and opened doors to more complex and challenging roles in both independent and mainstream cinema.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Supporting Actor – Brokeback Mountain (2006)
  • BAFTA Award for Best Supporting Actor – Brokeback Mountain (2006)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award Nomination – Brokeback Mountain (2006)
  • Critics' Choice Movie Award Nomination – Brokeback Mountain (2006)
  • Saturn Award for Best Actor – Donnie Darko (2002)
  • Hollywood Film Award for Actor of the Year – Nightcrawler (2014)
