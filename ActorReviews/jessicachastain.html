Jessica Chastain

Date of Birth: March 24, 1977

Location of Birth: Sacramento, California, USA

Height: 5'4" (163 cm)

Jessica Chastain is an Academy Award-winning actress known for her versatile performances and striking screen presence. With a career marked by critically acclaimed roles across various genres, she has established herself as one of the most respected and talented actresses of her generation.

Early Career

Chastain began her acting career in television, with guest roles in series like ER and Veronica Mars. She gained recognition for her stage work, particularly in a 2004 production of Rodney's Wife. Her breakthrough film role came in 2011 with multiple acclaimed performances in The Tree of Life, The Help, and Take Shelter. This remarkable year established her as a major talent in Hollywood and earned her her first Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress for The Help, launching her into international stardom.
