Itziar Ituño

Date of Birth: June 18, 1974

Location of Birth: Basauri, Basque Country, Spain

Height: 5'7" (170 cm)

Itziar Ituño is a Spanish actress best known for her role as Raquel Murillo (Lisbon) in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (La Casa de Papel). Known for her strong screen presence, emotional depth, and ability to portray complex female characters, she has become one of Spain's most respected actresses with a career spanning television, film, and theater.

Early Career

Itziar Ituño began her acting career in Basque-language television and theater. She gained recognition in the Basque Country for her role as Nekane Beitia in the long-running series Goenkale (2004-2015), where she appeared for over a decade. Her breakthrough to international audiences came with her portrayal of Inspector Raquel Murillo/Lisbon in Money Heist (2017-2021). Her character's transformation from a dedicated police inspector to a member of the heist crew became one of the series' most compelling arcs. Ituño's performance showcased her ability to convey strength, vulnerability, and moral complexity, earning her worldwide recognition. Beyond acting, she is also known for her activism and commitment to social causes.
