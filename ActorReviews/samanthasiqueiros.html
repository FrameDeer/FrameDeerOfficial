Samantha Siquiera

Date of Birth: August 12, 1994

Location of Birth: São Paulo, Brazil

Height: 5'6" (168 cm)

Samantha Siquiera is a Brazilian actress and model who gained international recognition for her role as Isabela in the Netflix series 3%. Known for her captivating screen presence and versatility, she has become one of Brazil's most promising young actresses with a growing international career.

Early Career

Samantha Siquiera began her career as a model before transitioning to acting. Her breakthrough came with her role in the internationally acclaimed Netflix series 3% (2016-2020), where she played Isabela, a character that showcased her dramatic range and established her as a rising star in Brazilian cinema. Her performance in the dystopian series earned her critical acclaim and international recognition. Siquiera has since expanded her career with roles in both Brazilian and international productions, demonstrating her ability to work across different genres and languages. Her background in modeling has contributed to her strong screen presence and ability to convey complex emotions through subtle expressions.
