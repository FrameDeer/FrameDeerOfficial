George Clooney

Date of Birth: May 6, 1961

Location of Birth: Lexington, Kentucky, U.S.

Height: 5'11" (180 cm)

George Clooney is an Academy Award-winning actor, director, producer, and humanitarian known for his charismatic screen presence and successful transition from television heartthrob to respected filmmaker. With a career spanning over three decades, he has established himself as one of Hollywood's most influential figures, equally acclaimed for his acting and his work behind the camera.

Early Career

George Clooney began his acting career in the early 1980s with small roles in television series and films. He struggled for years with bit parts before landing his breakthrough role as Dr. Doug Ross on the hit medical drama ER (1994-1999), which made him a household name and established him as a television heartthrob. His transition to film stardom came with roles in From Dusk Till Dawn (1996) and One Fine Day (1996), but it was his performance in Out of Sight (1998) that demonstrated his leading man potential in feature films. The success continued with the commercial hit The Perfect Storm (2000) and his role in the Coen Brothers' O Brother, Where Art Thou? (2000), establishing him as a versatile actor capable of both blockbuster entertainment and critically acclaimed cinema.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor – Syriana (2006)
  • Academy Award for Best Picture – Argo (2013) as producer
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – O Brother, Where Art Thou? (2001)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – The Descendants (2012)
  • BAFTA Award for Best Actor – Syriana (2006)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance – Syriana (2006)
  • Primetime Emmy Award – ER (1995)
  • Cecil B. DeMille Award for Lifetime Achievement (2015)
  • AFI Life Achievement Award (2018)
  • Academy Award nomination for Best Director – Good Night, and Good Luck (2006)
