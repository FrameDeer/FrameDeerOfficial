Brian Tyree Henry

Date of Birth: March 31, 1982

Location of Birth: Fayetteville, North Carolina, USA

Height: 6'2" (188 cm)

Brian Tyree Henry is an Academy Award-nominated American actor renowned for his powerful screen presence, emotional depth, and remarkable versatility across television, film, and theater. Known for his ability to bring authenticity and complexity to every role, he has established himself as one of the most compelling and respected character actors of his generation, equally adept at drama and comedy.

Early Career

Brian Tyree Henry began his career in theater, graduating from the Yale School of Drama and building an impressive stage career that included Broadway productions. His breakthrough came with his role as Alfred "Paper Boi" Miles in the critically acclaimed FX series Atlanta (2016-2022), which earned him an Emmy nomination and widespread recognition. He quickly became one of Hollywood's most sought-after character actors, delivering memorable performances in films like Widows (2018), If Beale Street Could Talk (2018), and Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018). His career reached new heights with his Academy Award-nominated performance in Causeway (2022), cementing his status as a formidable dramatic talent while continuing to excel in blockbuster films like Bullet Train and the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
