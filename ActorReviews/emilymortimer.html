Emily Mortimer

Date of Birth: December 1, 1971

Location of Birth: London, England

Height: 5'7" (170 cm)

Emily Mortimer is an English actress and screenwriter known for her intelligent, nuanced performances and distinctive voice. With a career spanning both British and American cinema, she has established herself as a versatile and respected character actress, equally adept at period dramas, romantic comedies, and complex independent films.

Early Career

Emily Mortimer began her acting career in the mid-1990s after studying at Oxford University. Her early work included roles in British television productions and stage work with the Royal Shakespeare Company. Her breakthrough came with her performance in the film The Last of the Mohicans (1992), though her role was ultimately cut from the final version. She gained wider recognition for her role in Notting Hill (1999) and particularly for her acclaimed performance in Lovely & Amazing (2001). Her career continued to flourish with notable roles in films like Match Point (2005) and Shutter Island (2010), while also establishing herself on television with her role in HBO's The Newsroom (2012-2014). More recently, she created and starred in the HBO series The Pursuit of Love (2021), demonstrating her talents as both actress and writer.
