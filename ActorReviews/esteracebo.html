Esther Acebo

Date of Birth: January 19, 1983

Location of Birth: Madrid, Spain

Height: 5'6" (168 cm)

Esther Acebo is a Spanish actress and television presenter who gained international recognition for her role as Mónica Gaztambide (Stockholm) in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (La Casa de Papel). Known for her emotional depth, intelligence, and ability to portray complex character transformations, she has become one of Spain's most respected actresses.

Early Career

Esther Acebo began her career as a television presenter on Spanish television, hosting programs like Zapeando and El Intermedio before transitioning to acting. Her breakthrough acting role came with her portrayal of Mónica Gaztambide/Stockholm in Money Heist (2017-2021), where her character's journey from the mistress of the Royal Mint's director to a full member of the heist crew became one of the series' most compelling arcs. Acebo's performance showcased her ability to convey vulnerability, strength, and moral complexity, earning her international recognition. Prior to Money Heist, she had appeared in Spanish television series and films, but it was her role as Stockholm that established her as a global star and demonstrated her dramatic range.
