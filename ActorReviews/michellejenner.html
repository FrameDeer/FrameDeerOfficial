Michelle Jenner

Date of Birth: September 14, 1986

Location of Birth: Barcelona, Spain

Height: 5'7" (170 cm)

Michelle Jenner is a Spanish actress known for her versatile performances across television, film, and voice acting. Gaining international recognition for her role as Keila in the Netflix series Berlin, she has established herself as one of Spain's most talented and respected actresses with a career spanning over two decades.

Early Career

Michelle Jenner began her acting career as a child, appearing in Spanish television series in the late 1990s. Her breakthrough came with her role as Sara in the popular Spanish series Los Serrano (2003-2008), which made her a household name in Spain. She further demonstrated her range with her acclaimed portrayal of Queen Isabella I of Castile in the historical series Isabel (2012-2014), earning critical praise for her powerful performance. Jenner's international breakthrough came with her role as Keila, the brilliant hacker, in the Netflix series Berlin (2023), the Money Heist spin-off that introduced her to global audiences and showcased her ability to portray intelligent, complex characters.
