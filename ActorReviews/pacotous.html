Paco Tous

Date of Birth: December 1, 1964

Location of Birth: Seville, Spain

Height: 5'10" (178 cm)

Paco Tous is a veteran Spanish actor known for his extensive career in Spanish cinema, television, and theatre. With decades of experience and numerous acclaimed performances, he has become one of Spain's most respected character actors, particularly recognized for his roles in popular Spanish series and his international breakthrough in Money Heist.

Early Career

Paco Tous began his acting career in the 1990s, building his reputation through numerous roles in Spanish television series and films. He gained significant recognition for his role as El Culebra in the popular Spanish series Los hombres de Paco (2005-2010), which established him as a household name in Spain. His career spans over three decades, during which he has worked consistently in Spanish cinema, television, and theatre. Tous gained international fame later in his career when he joined the cast of Money Heist as Moscow, bringing depth and warmth to the character that resonated with global audiences. His extensive experience and versatility have made him one of the pillars of the Spanish acting community.
