Julio Peña

Date of Birth: July 15, 2000

Location of Birth: Madrid, Spain

Height: 6'0" (183 cm)

Julio Peña is a rising Spanish actor known for his charismatic screen presence and rapid ascent in the Spanish entertainment industry. Gaining prominence through his roles in popular Spanish teen dramas and his recent international breakthrough in the Money Heist universe, he has established himself as one of Spain's most promising young talents with significant international appeal.

Early Career

Julio Peña began his acting career in Spanish television, quickly making a name for himself in youth-oriented programming. He gained significant recognition for his role as Iván Carvalho in the hit Disney Channel Spain series Bia (2019-2020), which established him as a teen idol and showcased his talent to a young audience. His career expanded with roles in series like Go! Live Your Way and various Spanish television projects. Peña achieved international recognition when he joined the cast of the Money Heist spin-off Berlín, marking his transition from teen-oriented content to more mature, globally-focused productions. His performance demonstrated his growing range and positioned him for continued success in both Spanish and international markets.
