Florence Pugh

Date of Birth: January 3, 1996

Location of Birth: Oxford, England

Height: 5'4" (163 cm)

Florence Pugh is an Academy Award-nominated English actress renowned for her powerful performances, emotional depth, and remarkable versatility across independent films and major blockbusters. Known for her fearless approach to complex characters and distinctive screen presence, she has quickly established herself as one of the most talented and sought-after actors of her generation.

Early Career

Florence Pugh began her acting career in 2014 with a role in the mystery drama The Falling. Her breakthrough came in 2016 with her leading performance in the independent film Lady Macbeth, for which she received critical acclaim and won the British Independent Film Award for Best Actress. This was followed by notable roles in The Little Drummer Girl (2018) miniseries and Fighting with My Family (2019). Her international breakthrough came with Greta Gerwig's Little Women (2019), earning her an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress. Since then, she has joined the Marvel Cinematic Universe as Yelena Belova and continued to choose challenging roles in films like Midsommar and Don't Worry Darling.
