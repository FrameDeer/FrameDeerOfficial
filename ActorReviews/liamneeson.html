Liam Neeson

Date of Birth: June 7, 1952

Location of Birth: Ballymena, Northern Ireland

Height: 6'4" (193 cm)

Liam Neeson is an Academy Award-nominated actor known for his commanding screen presence and distinctive voice. With a career spanning over four decades, he has established himself as one of Hollywood's most respected and versatile actors, equally adept at dramatic roles and action films.

Early Career

Neeson began his acting career on stage with the Lyric Players' Theatre in Belfast and later with the Abbey Theatre in Dublin. His film career started with small roles in British films, including Excalibur (1981). He gained recognition for his performance in The Bounty (1984) and The Mission (1986), but his breakthrough came with his Oscar-nominated role as Oskar Schindler in Schindler's List (1993), which established him as a leading dramatic actor in Hollywood and opened doors to major roles in both independent and mainstream cinema.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actor – Schindler's List (1994)
  • BAFTA Award Nomination for Best Actor – Schindler's List (1994)
  • Golden Globe Award Nomination for Best Actor – Schindler's List (1994)
  • Tony Award Nomination for Best Actor – Anna Christie (1993)
  • Officer of the Order of the British Empire (2000)
  • Irish Film & Television Academy Lifetime Achievement Award (2018)
