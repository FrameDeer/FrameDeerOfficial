Awkwafina

Date of Birth: June 2, 1988

Location of Birth: Stony Brook, New York, USA

Height: 5'3" (160 cm)

Awkwafina (born Nora Lum) is a Golden Globe-winning American actress, rapper, and comedian known for her distinctive voice, sharp comedic timing, and remarkable versatility across comedy and drama. Breaking barriers as one of the most prominent Asian-American performers in Hollywood, she has successfully transitioned from viral rap sensation to acclaimed actress, showcasing her range in both blockbuster films and intimate independent projects.

Early Career

Awkwafina began her career as a rapper and internet personality, gaining viral fame with her 2012 comedy rap video "My Vag." She released her first studio album, Yellow Ranger, in 2014 and continued to build her music career while making her initial forays into acting. Her breakthrough in film came with supporting roles in Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising (2016) and Ocean's 8 (2018), but it was her scene-stealing performance as Goh Peik Lin in Crazy Rich Asians (2018) that brought her mainstream recognition. Her career reached new heights with her Golden Globe-winning dramatic performance in The Farewell (2019), proving her ability to excel in both comedy and serious roles. Since then, she has become a major star in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and headlined her own Comedy Central series.
