Alba Flores

Date of Birth: October 27, 1986

Location of Birth: Madrid, Spain

Height: 5'4" (163 cm)

Alba Flores is a Spanish actress who gained international recognition for her powerful portrayal of Ágata Jiménez (Nairobi) in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (La Casa de Papel). Known for her intense screen presence, emotional depth, and distinctive style, she has become one of Spain's most beloved actresses, celebrated for her ability to bring strength and vulnerability to her characters.

Early Career

Alba Flores comes from a renowned acting family - she is the granddaughter of actress Lola Flores and daughter of singer and actress Lolita Flores. She began her acting career in Spanish television, appearing in series like El síndrome de Ulises and Aída. Her breakthrough came with her role as Saray in the critically acclaimed series Vis a Vis (Locked Up) (2015-2019), where she showcased her ability to portray complex, tough characters with hidden vulnerability. Her international stardom was cemented with her portrayal of Nairobi in Money Heist (2017-2021), where her character became one of the most beloved in the series. Flores' performance combined fierce determination with emotional depth, making Nairobi a fan favorite and establishing Flores as a global star.
