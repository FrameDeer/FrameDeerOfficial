Emma Stone

Date of Birth: November 6, 1988

Location of Birth: Scottsdale, Arizona, USA

Height: 5'6" (168 cm)

Emma Stone is an Academy Award-winning actress known for her versatility and charismatic screen presence. Rising to fame with comedic roles, she has since demonstrated remarkable range in both dramatic and musical performances, establishing herself as one of Hollywood's most talented and beloved actresses.

Early Career

Emma Stone began her acting career in theater productions during her childhood in Arizona. At age 15, she convinced her parents to let her move to Los Angeles to pursue acting, creating a "Project Hollywood" PowerPoint presentation to make her case. Her first television roles were in guest appearances on shows like Medium and Malcolm in the Middle. Her breakthrough came with the teen comedy Superbad (2007), which launched her film career and established her as a rising comedic talent in Hollywood.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Actress – La La Land (2017)
  • Academy Award for Best Actress – Poor Things (2024)
  • BAFTA Award for Best Actress – La La Land (2017)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – La La Land (2017)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Poor Things (2024)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award – The Help (2012)
  • Volpi Cup for Best Actress – Poor Things (2023)
