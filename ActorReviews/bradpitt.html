Brad Pitt

Date of Birth: December 18, 1963

Location of Birth: Shawnee, Oklahoma, USA

Height: 5'11" (180 cm)

Brad Pitt is an Academy Award-winning actor and film producer known for his versatility, charismatic screen presence, and remarkable career longevity. As one of Hollywood's most prominent leading men for over three decades, he has demonstrated exceptional range across genres while also establishing himself as a successful producer through his company Plan B Entertainment.

Early Career

Brad Pitt moved to Los Angeles just two weeks before graduating from college to pursue acting. He supported himself by driving strippers in a limousine, dressing as a giant chicken for El Pollo Loco, and working as a furniture mover. His early acting career consisted of small television roles on shows like Dallas and 21 Jump Street. His breakthrough came with a supporting role in Thelma & Louise (1991), where his brief but memorable performance as J.D. launched him to stardom. This was followed by leading roles in A River Runs Through It (1992) and Legends of the Fall (1994), establishing him as a major Hollywood leading man.
