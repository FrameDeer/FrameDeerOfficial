Begoña Vargas

Date of Birth: November 17, 1997

Location of Birth: Seville, Spain

Height: 5'6" (168 cm)

Begoña Vargas is a rising Spanish actress known for her captivating screen presence and rapid ascent in the Spanish entertainment industry. Gaining prominence through her roles in popular Spanish series and her recent international breakthrough in the Money Heist universe, she has established herself as one of Spain's most promising young talents.

Early Career

Begoña Vargas began her acting career in Spanish television, quickly making a name for herself with her natural talent and striking presence. She gained significant recognition for her role as Soledad "Sole" Castro in the hit Spanish period drama La Templanza (2021), which showcased her ability to handle complex historical roles. Her career accelerated with roles in series like Señoras del (h)AMPA and La Caza. Tramuntana. Vargas achieved international recognition when she joined the cast of the Money Heist spin-off Berlín, where her performance demonstrated her growing range and international appeal. Despite her young age, she has already demonstrated remarkable versatility across different genres and periods.
