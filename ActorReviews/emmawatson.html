Emma Watson

Date of Birth: April 15, 1990

Location of Birth: Paris, France

Height: 5'5" (165 cm)

Emma Watson is a British actress and activist best known for her role as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter film series. Beyond her iconic franchise work, she has established herself as a talented performer in various genres while also becoming a prominent advocate for gender equality and education.

Early Career

Emma Watson began her acting career at age nine when she was cast as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter film series, a role she would play for ten years across eight films. Before being cast, she had only performed in school plays. The first Harry Potter film, Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (2001), launched her to international fame at just eleven years old. She balanced her education with filming throughout her childhood and teenage years, attending school on set and eventually graduating from Brown University with a degree in English literature.

Major Awards

  • Young Artist Award for Best Ensemble – Harry Potter (2001)
  • Teen Choice Award for Choice Movie Actress – Harry Potter (2011)
  • MTV Movie Award for Best Female Performance – Harry Potter (2012)
  • San Diego Film Critics Society Award for Best Ensemble – The Perks of Being a Wallflower (2012)
  • Empire Hero Award (2014)
  • Best British Artist of the Year (2014)
