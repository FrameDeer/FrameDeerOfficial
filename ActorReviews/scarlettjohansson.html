Scarlett Johansson

Date of Birth: November 22, 1984

Location of Birth: Manhattan, New York City, U.S.

Height: 5'3" (160 cm)

Scarlett Johansson is one of the highest-grossing actresses of all time, known for her versatility in both independent films and major blockbuster franchises. With a career spanning from childhood to becoming a global superstar, she has demonstrated remarkable range in dramatic, comedic, and action roles.

Early Career

Scarlett Johansson began acting as a child, studying at the Lee Strasberg Theatre Institute and making her film debut at age 9 in North (1994). She gained critical acclaim for her performances in Manny & Lo (1996) and The Horse Whisperer (1998). Her breakthrough came with Sofia Coppola's Lost in Translation (2003), for which she won a BAFTA Award and received widespread recognition. This was followed by another acclaimed performance in Girl with a Pearl Earring (2003), establishing her as a serious dramatic actress before she transitioned into major franchise roles.

Major Awards

  • BAFTA Award for Best Actress – Lost in Translation (2004)
  • Tony Award for Best Featured Actress in a Play – A View from the Bridge (2010)
  • MTV Movie Award for Best Fight – Avengers: Endgame (2019)
  • People's Choice Award for Favorite Movie Actress (multiple years)
  • Academy Award nominations for Best Actress – Marriage Story (2020) and Best Supporting Actress – Jojo Rabbit (2020)
  • Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Actress in an Action Movie – Lucy (2015)
