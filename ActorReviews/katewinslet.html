Kate Winslet

Date of Birth: October 5, 1975

Location of Birth: Reading, Berkshire, England

Height: 5'7" (170 cm)

Kate Winslet is an Academy Award-winning English actress known for her versatile performances in both period dramas and contemporary films. With a career spanning over three decades, she has established herself as one of the most respected and accomplished actresses of her generation, renowned for her emotional depth and technical skill.

Early Career

Winslet began her career in British television in the early 1990s, with her breakthrough film role coming in Peter Jackson's Heavenly Creatures (1994). Her performance caught the attention of international audiences and led to her being cast as Marianne Dashwood in Sense and Sensibility (1995), which earned her first Academy Award nomination. However, it was her role as Rose DeWitt Bukater in Titanic (1997) that catapulted her to global stardom and made her one of the most sought-after actresses in Hollywood.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Actress – The Reader (2008)
  • BAFTA Award for Best Actress – The Reader (2008)
  • BAFTA Award for Best Actress – Steve Jobs (2015)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Revolutionary Road (2008)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Steve Jobs (2015)
  • Primetime Emmy Award – Mildred Pierce (2011)
  • Grammy Award for Best Audio Book – Listen to the Storyteller (1999)
  • European Film Award for Best Actress – The Reader (2008)
