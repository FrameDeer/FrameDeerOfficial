Matthew McConaughey

Date of Birth: November 4, 1969

Location of Birth: Uvalde, Texas, USA

Height: 6'0" (183 cm)

Matthew McConaughey is an Academy Award-winning actor known for his distinctive Southern charm and remarkable career transformation. After early success in romantic comedies, he underwent a career renaissance known as the "McConaissance" with critically acclaimed dramatic performances that established him as one of Hollywood's most respected actors.

Early Career

Matthew McConaughey began his acting career in the early 1990s after moving to Los Angeles. His breakthrough came with a supporting role in Richard Linklater's Dazed and Confused (1993), where his memorable line "alright, alright, alright" became iconic. He quickly transitioned to leading man status with roles in A Time to Kill (1996) and Contact (1997). Throughout the late 1990s and 2000s, he became known for romantic comedies like How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days (2003) and Failure to Launch (2006), before his dramatic career resurgence in the 2010s.
