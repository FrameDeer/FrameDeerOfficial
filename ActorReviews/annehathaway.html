Anne Hathaway

Date of Birth: November 12, 1982

Location of Birth: Brooklyn, New York, USA

Height: 5'8" (173 cm)

Anne Hathaway is an Academy Award-winning actress known for her versatility across genres, from romantic comedies to intense dramatic roles. With a career spanning over two decades, she has established herself as one of Hollywood's most talented and respected actresses, celebrated for her emotional depth and charismatic screen presence.

Early Career

Anne Hathaway began her acting career in television series before landing her breakthrough role as Mia Thermopolis in The Princess Diaries (2001). Her early success in family-friendly films established her as a rising star, but she quickly demonstrated her range by taking on more mature roles. The transition to serious acting came with her acclaimed performance in Brokeback Mountain (2005), followed by her Oscar-nominated role in Rachel Getting Married (2008), which proved her ability to handle complex, dramatic material and marked her evolution into a respected dramatic actress.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress – Les Misérables (2013)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actress – Les Misérables (2013)
  • BAFTA Award for Best Actress in a Supporting Role – Les Misérables (2013)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance – Les Misérables (2013)
  • Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Voice-Over Performance – The Simpsons (2010)
  • National Board of Review Award for Best Cast – Les Misérables (2012)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actress – Rachel Getting Married (2008)
  • Golden Globe Nomination for Best Actress – Rachel Getting Married (2008)
  • BAFTA Rising Star Award (2009)
  • Critics' Choice Movie Award Nomination for Best Actress – The Devil Wears Prada (2006)
