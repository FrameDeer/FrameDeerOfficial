Will Poulter

Date of Birth: January 28, 1993

Location of Birth: Hammersmith, London, England

Height: 6'2" (188 cm)

Will Poulter is a BAFTA-winning English actor known for his distinctive appearance, remarkable physical transformations, and impressive range across comedy, drama, and action genres. From his early comedic roles to his recent dramatic and physically demanding performances, he has established himself as one of Britain's most versatile and compelling young actors.

Early Career

Will Poulter began his acting career as a teenager, making his film debut in Son of Rambow (2007) at age 14. His breakthrough came with his acclaimed performance as Lee Carter in The Chronicles of Narnia: The Voyage of the Dawn Treader (2010). He gained widespread recognition for his comedic role in We're the Millers (2013), which showcased his timing and earned him an MTV Movie Award. Poulter deliberately shifted to more serious roles with his BAFTA-winning performance in Wild Bill (2011) and his intense turn in The Revenant (2015). His career has continued to evolve with roles in Detroit (2017), Midsommar (2019), and his recent entry into the Marvel Cinematic Universe, demonstrating his commitment to challenging and diverse projects.
