Zendaya

Date of Birth: September 1, 1996

Location of Birth: Oakland, California, USA

Height: 5'10" (178 cm)

Zendaya is an Emmy Award-winning American actress, singer, and producer who has rapidly become one of Hollywood's most influential young stars. Known for her sophisticated fashion sense, powerful performances, and advocacy for diversity and inclusion, she has successfully transitioned from Disney Channel star to acclaimed dramatic actress, establishing herself as a leading voice of her generation.

Early Career

Zendaya began her career as a child model and backup dancer, appearing in commercials for brands like Old Navy and Macy's. Her breakthrough came in 2010 when she was cast as Rocky Blue in the Disney Channel series Shake It Up, which ran for three seasons and introduced her to a young audience. During this time, she also pursued music, releasing her debut single "Replay" in 2013. Her transition to more mature roles began with her lead role in the Disney Channel series K.C. Undercover (2015-2018), which she also co-produced. However, her career-defining moment came with her casting as MJ in the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Spider-Man series and her groundbreaking performance as Rue Bennett in HBO's Euphoria, which earned her two Emmy Awards and established her as a serious dramatic actress.
