Angelina Jolie

Date of Birth: June 4, 1975

Location of Birth: Los Angeles, California, USA

Height: 5'7" (170 cm)

Angelina Jolie is an Academy Award-winning actress known for her powerful performances and distinctive screen presence. With a career spanning over three decades, she has established herself as one of Hollywood's most versatile and compelling actresses, appearing in both critically acclaimed dramas and major blockbuster films.

Early Career

Jolie began her acting career in the mid-1990s with roles in low-budget films, including Cyborg 2 (1993) and Hackers (1995). Her breakthrough came with the television films George Wallace (1997) and Gia (1998), for which she won Golden Globe Awards and critical acclaim for her intense performances. However, it was her Oscar-winning role as Lisa Rowe in Girl, Interrupted (1999) that catapulted her to international stardom and established her as one of Hollywood's most compelling and daring actresses.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress – Girl, Interrupted (2000)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actress – Girl, Interrupted (2000)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Gia (1999)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – George Wallace (1998)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Best Supporting Actress – Girl, Interrupted (2000)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance – Gia (1999)
