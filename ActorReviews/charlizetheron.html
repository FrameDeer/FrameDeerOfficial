Charlize Theron

Date of Birth: August 7, 1975

Location of Birth: Benoni, South Africa

Height: 5'10" (177 cm)

Charlize Theron is an Academy Award-winning actress and producer known for her fearless transformations and powerful performances in both dramatic and action roles. From her early modeling career to becoming one of Hollywood's most respected actresses, she has demonstrated remarkable range and commitment to her craft.

Early Career

Charlize Theron began her career as a model in her native South Africa before moving to the United States to pursue acting. After studying at the Joffrey Ballet School in New York, an injury ended her dance career and she transitioned to acting in Los Angeles. Her film debut came in Children of the Corn III: Urban Harvest (1995), but her breakthrough role was in 2 Days in the Valley (1996). She gained wider recognition with performances in The Devil's Advocate (1997) and Mighty Joe Young (1998). However, it was her transformative performance as serial killer Aileen Wuornos in Monster (2003) that catapulted her to critical acclaim, earning her an Academy Award for Best Actress and establishing her as a serious dramatic actress willing to undergo radical physical transformations for her roles.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Actress – Monster (2004)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Monster (2004)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance – Monster (2004)
  • Silver Bear for Best Actress – Monster (2004)
  • Academy Award nomination for Best Actress – North Country (2006)
  • Academy Award nomination for Best Actress – Bombshell (2020)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Bombshell (2020)
  • Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Actress – Mad Max: Fury Road (2016)
  • Hollywood Walk of Fame Star (2005)
  • MTV Movie Award for Best Fight – Atomic Blonde (2018)
