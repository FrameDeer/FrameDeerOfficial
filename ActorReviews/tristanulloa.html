Tristán Ulloa

Date of Birth: May 6, 1970

Location of Birth: Vigo, Pontevedra, Spain

Height: 6'0" (183 cm)

Tristán Ulloa is a Spanish actor, film director, and screenwriter known for his extensive career in Spanish cinema and television. With decades of experience and numerous acclaimed performances, he has established himself as one of Spain's most versatile and respected talents, equally adept at acting, writing, and directing across various genres.

Early Career

Tristán Ulloa began his acting career in the early 1990s, quickly establishing himself as a prominent figure in Spanish cinema. He gained significant recognition for his role in Alejandro Amenábar's breakthrough film Open Your Eyes (1997), which brought him international attention. His career expanded with roles in films like Lucía y el sexo (2001) and numerous Spanish television series. Ulloa has also established himself as a talented director and screenwriter, with films like Salir pitando (2007) showcasing his multifaceted talents. His recent work in the Money Heist spin-off Berlín demonstrates his continued relevance and ability to connect with new generations of audiences while maintaining his artistic integrity.
