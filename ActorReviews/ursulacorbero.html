Úrsula Corberó

Date of Birth: August 11, 1989

Location of Birth: Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain

Height: 5'4" (162 cm)

Úrsula Corberó is a Spanish actress who achieved international stardom for her role as Silene Oliveira (Tokyo) in the global Netflix phenomenon Money Heist (La Casa de Papel). Known for her fiery screen presence, distinctive style, and emotional intensity, she has become one of Spain's most recognizable actresses worldwide and a fashion icon.

Early Career

Úrsula Corberó began her acting career as a child, appearing in Catalan television series and commercials. Her breakthrough came with the teen series Física o Química (2008-2011), where she played Ruth Gómez for four seasons. She continued to build her career in Spanish television with roles in series like Isabel and La embajada. However, her international breakthrough arrived with her portrayal of Tokyo in Money Heist (2017-2021), which became Netflix's most-watched non-English language series. Her performance as the impulsive, passionate narrator of the heist earned her worldwide recognition and established her as a global star. Following this success, she has expanded into international projects while maintaining her presence in Spanish cinema and television.
