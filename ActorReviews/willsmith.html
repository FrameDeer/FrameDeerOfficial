Will Smith

Date of Birth: September 25, 1968

Location of Birth: Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA

Height: 6'2" (188 cm)

Will Smith is an Academy Award-winning actor, rapper, and producer who rose to fame as a hip-hop artist before becoming one of Hollywood's biggest movie stars. Known for his charismatic screen presence and ability to excel in both action blockbusters and dramatic roles, he has consistently been one of the most bankable stars in the industry.

Early Career

Will Smith began his entertainment career as a rapper, forming the duo DJ Jazzy Jeff & The Fresh Prince with childhood friend Jeffrey Townes. They achieved massive success in the late 1980s with hits like "Parents Just Don't Understand" and "Summertime," winning the first Grammy Award for Best Rap Performance in 1989. His television breakthrough came with the NBC sitcom The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air (1990-1996), which launched him to mainstream fame. He transitioned to film with Where the Day Takes You (1992) and Made in America (1993), but his major film breakthrough came with Bad Boys (1995) and Independence Day (1996), establishing him as a major Hollywood star.
