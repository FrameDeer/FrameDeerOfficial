Tom Cruise

Date of Birth: July 3, 1962

Location of Birth: Syracuse, New York, USA

Height: 5'7" (170 cm)

Tom Cruise is one of Hollywood's most successful and recognizable actors, known for his intense dedication to performing his own stunts and his roles in blockbuster action films. With a career spanning over four decades, he has become one of the highest-grossing movie stars of all time and is particularly famous for his Mission: Impossible franchise.

Early Career

Tom Cruise began his acting career in the early 1980s with supporting roles in films like Endless Love (1981) and Taps (1981). His breakthrough came with the teen comedy Risky Business (1983), where his performance as a charismatic teenager and his iconic dance scene in underwear made him a rising star. He quickly transitioned to leading man status with roles in Top Gun (1986) and The Color of Money (1986), establishing himself as one of Hollywood's most bankable young actors by the mid-1980s.
