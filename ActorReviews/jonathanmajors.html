Jonathan Majors

Date of Birth: September 7, 1989

Location of Birth: Lompoc, California, USA

Height: 6'0" (183 cm)

Jonathan Majors is an American actor known for his intense, physically commanding performances and remarkable emotional range. Rising rapidly to prominence through critically acclaimed independent films and major franchise roles, he established himself as one of Hollywood's most promising talents before his career was impacted by legal controversies in 2023.

Early Career

Jonathan Majors began his acting career after graduating from the University of North Carolina School of the Arts, with his professional debut in the 2017 film Hostiles. His breakthrough came with his powerful performance as a troubled teenager in The Last Black Man in San Francisco (2019), which earned him critical acclaim and established him as a rising star. This was followed by his role in the HBO series Lovecraft Country (2020), which brought him mainstream recognition and an Emmy nomination. His career trajectory accelerated with roles in major productions including Da 5 Bloods (2020), The Harder They Fall (2021), and his casting as Kang the Conqueror in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, beginning with Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania (2023). However, his promising career faced significant challenges following legal issues in 2023 that impacted his professional relationships and future projects.
