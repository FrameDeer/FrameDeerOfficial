Johnny Depp

Date of Birth: June 9, 1963

Location of Birth: Owensboro, Kentucky, U.S.

Height: 5'10" (178 cm)

Johnny Depp is one of Hollywood's most versatile and unconventional actors, known for his eccentric character choices and collaborations with director Tim Burton. With a career spanning over three decades, he has established himself as a master of transformation, bringing unique and often bizarre characters to life with remarkable depth and commitment.

Early Career

Johnny Depp began his acting career in the 1980s, first gaining recognition as a teenage heartthrob on the television series 21 Jump Street (1987-1990). Eager to break away from his pin-up image, he pursued unconventional film roles starting with A Nightmare on Elm Street (1984). His collaboration with director Tim Burton began with Edward Scissorhands (1990), which established his reputation as a serious actor capable of portraying complex, outsider characters. Throughout the 1990s, Depp continued to choose eclectic roles in independent films like Benny & Joon (1993), Ed Wood (1994), and Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas (1998), cementing his status as one of Hollywood's most daring and unpredictable actors.

Major Awards

  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street (2008)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance – Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl (2004)
  • People's Choice Award for Favorite Movie Actor (multiple years)
  • MTV Movie Award for Best Performance – Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl (2004)
  • Academy Award nomination for Best Actor – Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl (2004)
  • BAFTA Award nomination for Best Actor – Finding Neverland (2005)
  • Volpi Cup for Best Actor – Don Juan DeMarco (1995)
