Mark Ruffalo

Date of Birth: November 22, 1967

Location of Birth: Kenosha, Wisconsin, USA

Height: 5'8" (173 cm)

Mark Ruffalo is an Academy Award-nominated actor known for his naturalistic performances and versatility across independent films and major blockbusters. Best known for playing Bruce Banner/The Hulk in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, he has also delivered critically acclaimed performances in numerous dramatic roles.

Early Career

Mark Ruffalo began his acting career in the early 1990s, performing in numerous stage productions and small theater companies in Los Angeles. He co-founded the Orpheus Theatre Company and appeared in several off-Broadway productions. His breakthrough film role came in Kenneth Lonergan's You Can Count On Me (2000), for which he received critical acclaim and several award nominations. This performance established him as a talented character actor and opened doors to more significant roles in both independent films and mainstream Hollywood productions.
