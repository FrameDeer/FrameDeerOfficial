Timothée Chalamet

Date of Birth: December 27, 1995

Location of Birth: New York City, New York, USA

Height: 5'10" (178 cm)

Timothée Chalamet is an Academy Award-nominated American actor renowned for his intense, emotionally raw performances and sophisticated choice of roles. Often described as the defining actor of his generation, he has quickly established himself as one of Hollywood's most sought-after leading men through his work in critically acclaimed independent films and major studio productions.

Early Career

Timothée Chalamet began his acting career in television, appearing in episodes of Law & Order and Homeland while studying at Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts. His film debut came with a small role in Men, Women & Children (2014), followed by his breakthrough performance as a teenage boy experiencing first love in Luca Guadagnino's Call Me by Your Name (2017). This role earned him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor, making him the third-youngest nominee in that category. He further demonstrated his range with supporting roles in Lady Bird (2017) and Little Women (2019), before transitioning to leading man status with films like Beautiful Boy (2018) and establishing himself as a box office draw with major franchise films like Dune (2021).
